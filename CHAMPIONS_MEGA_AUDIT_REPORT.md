## New Abilities Summary

Four abilities are entirely new to Pokémon Champions. All four debut on Champions-introduced Megas.

| Ability | Holder | Effect Summary |
|---------|--------|----------------|
| **Piercing Drill** | Mega Excadrill | Contact moves deal 25% damage through protection; all non-protective effects still trigger. Functionally the renamed version of the nerfed Unseen Fist for new Megas. Sources: [Serebii](https://www.serebii.net/pokemonchampions/newabilities.shtml), [Game8](https://game8.co/games/Pokemon-Champions/archives/590403) |
| **Dragonize** | Mega Feraligatr | Normal-type moves become Dragon-type with +20% power boost. Combined with Water/Dragon STAB, this enables massive damage output. Sources: [Serebii](https://www.serebii.net/pokemonchampions/newabilities.shtml), [Game8](https://game8.co/games/Pokemon-Champions/archives/590403) |
| **Mega Sol** | Mega Meganium | The holder treats all moves as if harsh sunlight were active, without triggering actual Drought weather. Fire moves get x1.5 boost; Solar Beam requires no charge; Chlorophyll/Flower Gift activate for holder. Water-type opponents do NOT take extra damage (not real sun). Sources: [Serebii](https://www.serebii.net/pokemonchampions/newabilities.shtml), [Game8](https://game8.co/games/Pokemon-Champions/archives/590403) |
| **Spicy Spray** | Mega Scovillain | When the holder takes any damage from a move, the attacker is burned. Unlike Flame Body, requires no contact — any damaging move triggers it. Fire-type attackers are immune to burn. Sources: [Serebii](https://www.serebii.net/pokemonchampions/newabilities.shtml), [Game8](https://game8.co/games/Pokemon-Champions/archives/590403) |

## Updated Abilities (Behavior Changes from Prior Games)

| Ability | Original Behavior | Champions Behavior | Who is Affected |
|---------|------------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **Unseen Fist** | Contact moves deal **100% full damage** through Protect/Detect | Contact moves deal **25% damage** through protection; non-protective effects still trigger | Mega Golurk (new Mega user), all Urshifu forms | 
| **Parental Bond** | Second hit at **1/2 power** (Gen 6 original) | Second hit at **1/4 power** | Mega Kangaskhan |
| **Protean** | Changed type **every move** used | Changes type **once per entry** | Mega Greninja |

**Sources for Unseen Fist nerf:** [Serebii Updated Abilities](https://www.serebii.net/pokemonchampions/updatedabilities.shtml), [Game8 Abilities](https://game8.co/games/Pokemon-Champions/archives/590403), [Reddit community discussion](https://www.reddit.com/r/PokemonChampions/comments/1sh4vf0/was_unseen_fist_really_the_problem_or_was_it_just/)

**Note on Parental Bond:** The 1/4 power nerf is stated in Game8's Champions Mega Kangaskhan description. The original Gen 6 value was 1/2. This is a significant meta impact.

**Note on Protean:** Game8 Champions abilities page explicitly states "This works only once per time the Pokémon enters battle." This nerf first appeared in Gen 9 (SV) and carries into Champions.

## Legality Notes: Regulation Set M-A

Per [Serebii Reg M-A](https://www.serebii.net/pokemonchampions/rankedbattle/regulationm-a.shtml) and [Victory Road](https://victoryroad.pro/champions-regulations/):

- **All 57 Megas in the dataset are confirmed legal in Regulation Set M-A** (all appear in the "Newly Useable Pokémon" table for Season M-1).
- **Mewtwo X/Y** — NOT in Reg M-A (Mewtwo likely banned as Restricted Legendary; Mega Stones not in item list).
- **Latias/Latios** — NOT confirmed in Reg M-A (not in Reg M-A newly useable list; Mega Stones not in item list).
- **Mega Chesnaught, Mega Delphox, Mega Greninja, Mega Floette** — Legal in Reg M-A but require HOME transfer to obtain.
- Regulation M-A season runs: April 8, 2026 → June 17, 2026.
- VGC events start May 2026 with Global Challenge (online) and Asian Nationals.
- Indianapolis Regional Championships is the first major TPCi Reg M-A event.
